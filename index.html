Welcome to Amaala Global, A Legacy of Trust Since 2002

Established in Saudi at 2002, by Saeed Al Ghamdi, Amaala Contracting Division which had taken its shape from the group company Ghadeer has grown from trusted contracting company to a unique, full-service design firm whose curation and expertise deliver unparalleled design ingenuity. Our all-inclusive solutions, and expertise to pick from a network of world-class names in architecture and engineering, ensure that our clients' projects meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Amaala Global's success is rooted in strong partnerships with globally renowned brands and a commitment to the highest professional standards. The group prioritizes employee development through robust policies and practices, recognizing its people as its greatest asset. Amaala Global is also dedicated to social responsibility, focusing first on internal initiatives before extending its efforts to the broader community.
