Welcome to Amaala Global, A Legacy of Trust Since 2002

Formerly known as Ghadeer Transportation, Amaala Global has grown from a transportation company into a leading provider of logistics, facility management, and prefabricated construction solutions. Since 2002, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, efficiency, and innovation, delivering seamless services that drive operational excellence.

With a commitment to sustainability and global partnerships, Amaala Global continues to set new standards in integrated solutions, ensuring quality and growth for businesses and communities alike.
